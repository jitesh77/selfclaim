Before you start…

We thank you for choosing to prepare your claim using SureClaim platform. We’ll try and make this process informative and convenient for you.

Just so you know, your claim form looks something like the form on the right-hand side. The format or layout of forms may vary as per insurance company or TPA, however, the data these forms collect remains the same.

Our research indicates that one needs to fill 40-60 fields in the claim form to prepare a complete claim. This means you need to be patient through this elaborate process. Click on the Help button next to the field to understand more about it.

We recommend you should keep following set of documents with you to fill relevant details in form:

  • Discharge Summary
  • Hospital Bill received at time of discharge
  • All other bills for pharmacy, diagnostics and consultations
  • Latest year’s Insurance policy certificate or insurance/TPA customer ID card

At any point if you need assistance or want to share a feedback or suggestion, message us right from the feedback window available to you on the screen.
